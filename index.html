Team up Cabinets

Team up Cabinets is a local, family-owned and operated company specializing in the design, manufacture and installation of quality custom cabinetry.

For over 40 years, Team up Cabinets has been serving Surrey, British Columbia from new builds and renovations to commercial space. Visit our showroom and talk to us about how we can create custom cabinet solutions to fit any style and space.

BATHROOM

Team up Cabinets supplies a wide range of cabinets to help achieve the look you desire. From the most simple two-door cabinet with a sink, to highly elaborate “furniture-look” custom cabinetry with matching mirror frames, storage towers, custom kicks and more, we have it all. It is a versatile manufacturer, supplying traditional and contemporary-style cabinetry.
